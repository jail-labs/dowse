Signals that predict hiring

The data points that matter, and why

Funding rounds

Companies hire aggressively after raising. Most add 30% headcount within six months of a round.

Leadership changes

New execs build their own teams. A new VP Sales means SDRs. A new CTO means engineers.

Office expansions

New locations mean new roles. Companies don't sign leases unless they plan to fill seats.

Headcount trends

We track LinkedIn employee counts weekly. Consistent growth predicts continued hiring.

Product launches

New products need teams to sell, support, and build them. Launches trigger hiring across the board.

Press coverage

Major press follows milestones. Milestones mean growth. Growth means hiring.
